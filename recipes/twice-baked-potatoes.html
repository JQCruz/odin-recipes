Delicious Twice Baked Potatoes

Twice baked potatoes are an easy and delicous side that works well with almost any dish!

  • Difficulty: 2
  • Taste: 5!
  • Vegan Friendly: Yes!!
  • Keto Friendly: Of Course!
  • Hail: Hydra

Ingredients

  • Russet Potatoes: 8lbs
  • Family Sized Bag of Mexican Cheese
  • Sour Cream: 1 Cup
  • Butter: 4Tbps
  • Milk: Eyeball it dumby

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Place Potatoes in a bowl with holes, rinse under water and thorouly clean them
  3. Evenly spread out potatoes on a baking sheet and bake until tender. About one hour
  4. Cut potatoes in halves. Scoop flesh of potatoes into a bowl, leaving enough in skins so that potatoes are like a sturdy potatoe boat.
  5. Mix in butter, milk, sour cream, and cheese.
  6. Scoop mixture into potato boats, try to fill in potatoes evenly.
  7. Cover potatoes with some more mexican cheese, and bake until cheese is golden brown
  8. Enjoy!
